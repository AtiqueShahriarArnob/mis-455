Shortly about Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a country in South Asia, bordered by India, Myanmar, and the Bay of Bengal. It gained independence in 1971 after a nine-month-long Liberation War. Since then, the country has made significant progress in agriculture, textiles, and technology.

With a population of over 160 million, Bangladesh is one of the world's most densely populated countries. It has a diverse cultural heritage, influenced by Hindu, Buddhist, and Islamic traditions. The country is famous for its rivers, Sundarbans mangrove forest, and historical landmarks such as the Lalbagh Fort and Somapura Mahavihara.
