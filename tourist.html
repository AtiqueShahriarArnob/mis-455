Tourist Attractions in Bangladesh

Bangladesh is home to breathtaking natural beauty, historical sites, and vibrant culture. Some of the most popular tourist destinations include Cox’s Bazar, Sundarbans, Srimangal, and the historic city of Bagerhat.

Cox’s Bazar is the longest unbroken sea beach in the world, attracting thousands of tourists every year. Learn more on the Bangladesh Tourism Board website.

The Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is famous for its Bengal tigers and diverse wildlife. More information can be found on UNESCO's official page.

Srimangal is known as the ‘Tea Capital of Bangladesh,’ offering lush green tea gardens and serene landscapes. Check out Bangladesh Tourism for more travel tips.
