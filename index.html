Welcome to Bangladesh

Bangladesh, located in South Asia, is known for its rich history, culture, and natural beauty. The country is home to the world's largest river delta and the stunning Sundarbans mangrove forest.

Dhaka, the capital city, is famous for its bustling streets, vibrant markets, and delicious cuisine. Cox's Bazar, the longest unbroken sea beach in the world, is a must-visit destination.
